Watch The Marvel Movies In Order

From 2008, the first Iron Man movie,
Marvel has set up a great superhero universe.
After 10 years, in 2019, Avengers 4 End Game moved me a lot.

As a Marvel movie fan, I watched all of those superhero movies. But if we want to know the correct time-line, let me tell you the right watching order.

1. Captain America:
The First Avenger (2011)
 2. Agent Carter Season 1 (2015)

4. Iron Man (2008)

5. The Incredible Hulk (2008)
